Introduction
A data structure is a specialized format for organizing and storing data.
For instance, let's say that you like piling/stacking up plates rather because of its properties(being flat and concave), which makes it easier to keep on shelves or tables etc.
This is nothing but a data structure, where you organized and stored the plates in a particular way to suit your needs.
